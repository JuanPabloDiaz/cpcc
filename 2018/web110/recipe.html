Cinnamon Rolls

INGREDIENTS:

Gather the following ingredients
PRODUCT QTY UNIT
Flour 200 Gr
Yeast 25 Gr
Milk 200 CC
Dough:
Flour 800 Gr
Salt 15 Gr
Sugar 200 Gr
Eggs 4 U.
Milk 200 CC
Butter 100 Gr
Filling:
Butter 80 Gr
Muscovado sugar 120 Gr
Cinnamon powder 25 Gr
Raisins 100 Gr
Glazed:
Apricot jam 1 Cup
Impactable sugar 200 Gr
Water 50 Cc
TOTAL (Mass) 1,890 GR

PROCESS:

Dough...

  1. Make a volcano with flour and mix with salt.
  2. Place the baking powder in the center.
  3. Add sugar, eggs one at a time and add half of the milk as necessary. Continue kneading from the center towards the edges: integrate the flour.
  4. Add the butter in cubes and continue kneading until you get a uniform texture.
  5. Incorporate the rest of the milk little by little and continue the kneading until achieving a texture that is detached from the work table.
  6. Form a ball with the dough and place in a slightly greased metal bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and reserve in a warm place until the dough doubles its volume.
  7. Flour the work table and form a regular rectangle 1 cm high in dough with the help of a rolling pin.

Filling...

  1. Spread the softened butter over the dough and sprinkle the mixture of mashed sugar and cinnamon. Spread the raisins.
  2. Roll up regularly and cut into pieces 6 cm long. Place each piece with the snail upwards in a mold greased with butter and reserve for 10 minutes before baking.

Glazed...

  1. Bake at 170 Â° C for approximately 25 minutes.
  2. To make it look more appetizing, paint with apricot glaze. Also prepare a little white glaze, mixing impalpable sugar and water. Apply on the loaves.
